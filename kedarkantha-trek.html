Description

Kedarkantha is the crown jewel of winter trekking in the Himalayas. Known for its perfect summit climb and breathtaking campsites, it is the ideal trek for those looking to experience the magic of snow-covered pine forests and high-altitude peaks.

The summit of Kedarkantha (12,500 ft) offers an unparalleled 360-degree view of the Great Himalayan ranges, including the Swargarohini, Black Peak, and Bandarpoonch mountains. The campsites at Kedarkantha, especially Juda ka Talab, are amongst the most picturesque in the world, with frozen lakes surrounded by towering trees under a star-studded sky.
