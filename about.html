About Mountain Adventure

Your Trusted Companion for Himalayan Explorations since 2016.

Story Behind Mountain Adventure

Mountain Adventure was created in 2016 by two young entrepreneurs with a passion for the mountains. What began as a small vision to bring adventure lovers together on a single platform has now grown into one of the most trusted names in Himalayan trekking.

Treks that we conducted 8 years ago for solo travelers became our inspiration. We managed everything from scratch, which gave us the spark to establish our own identity in the trekking world.

Guiding the Way

We are a group of trekkers and mountain lovers. The company was founded to symbolize the start of our independence to conduct business while upholding our social responsibility towards our people in the mountains.

Being mountain natives ourselves, we take pride in presenting the Raw Himalayas in their most authentic form, ensuring every trekker goes back with stories for a lifetime.

Our Vision

To help local people who lack opportunities in the mountains. We strive to support charitable causes and provide the safest, most transparent trekking experiences available in the Indian Himalayas.

Our Mission

Our team works dedicatedly with a common mission to serve our customers with the finest treks possible. We make sure that you get the best moments with us and book us for more amazing treks in the future.
