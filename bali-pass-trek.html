Description

Bali Pass is an adrenaline-fueled high-altitude crossover trek that connects the scenic Har Ki Dun valley to the holy site of Yamunotri. It is considered one of the more challenging treks in the region, featuring steep technical climbs, high-altitude passes, and massive snowfields.

Along the way, trekkers are rewarded with the stunning beauty of Ruinsara Tal, a pristine alpine lake, and close-up views of the Swargarohini and Bandarpoonch massifs. The final crossing of Bali Pass (16,200 ft) is a trial of endurance that culminates in spectacular panoramic views of the entire Garhwal range.

Itinerary

1

Dehradun to Sankri

Approx 200km drive, 8 hours.

2

Sankri to Seema

Drive to Taluka, then trek to Seema.

3

Seema to Rainbasera

Trek along the Supin river.

4

Rainbasera to Ruinsara Tal

Reach the beautiful alpine lake.

5

Ruinsara Tal to Odari

Trek towards the pass base camp.

6

Odari to Bali Pass Base Camp

Short trek to acclimatize.

7

Base Camp to Bali Pass & Lower Dhamni

Cross the difficult pass.

8

Lower Dhamni to Janki Chatti & Dehradun

Descend to Yamunotri and drive back.
